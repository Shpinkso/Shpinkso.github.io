How Can This Site Help You?

With 20 years in 8 different companies, writing software, leading teams and managing projects & programmes – I’ve learned a lot about what works and what definitely does not. This site is intended to share my experience around how to build and maintain great products and services, by guiding and nurturing the teams that create them. My experience is first and foremost in the embedded sector, which brings unique challenges to modern software development practices.
